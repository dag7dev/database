Puzzle Sky
by Par J 2002

Game Instructions

Your goal is to direct the little people to their homes. The little people
are trapped in the sky, and you have to jump and walk on clouds to reach them.
When you stand in front of a little guy you can toss him up, down, left or
right. When the little people are in their homes (A,B,C...), you have completed
the puzzle. Watch out for the flying star!


Controls

Walk left           Left
Walk right          Right
Jump                Button A
Jump down           Down
Toss little guy     Button B


Special Controls

Mega jump           Button A + Up
Jump far down       Hold down 'Down'
