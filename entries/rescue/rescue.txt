-----------------------------------
Rescue - 2002 Minigame Compo Entry
-----------------------------------

Author   : Tomasz Slanina  dox@space.pl
Platform : Gameboy Color

Don't shot, don't kill, don't eat -  rescue  ;)

Controls are :
 cursor keys - movement left/right

Difficulty increases with the number of rescued ppls.
Game has been tested on emulators and on a real GBC as well.
